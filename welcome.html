1 Get Your API Key

CivicTag uses Google's Gemini AI to understand your complaints.

Steps:

  1. Visit Google AI Studio
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Click "Create API Key"
  4. Copy the key
⚠️ Important: Keep your API key private! Don't share it publicly.

2 Configure the Extension

Click the CivicTag icon in your browser toolbar and:

  1. Go to the Settings tab
  2. Paste your Gemini API key
  3. Set your default state and city
  4. Click Save

3 Start Tagging Authorities!

Head to Twitter/X and compose a complaint tweet. CivicTag will:

  • ✅ Automatically detect your complaint
  • ✅ Suggest relevant government handles
  • ✅ Track responses and remind you to follow up

💡 Pro Tips

  • Be specific: Mention your city, area, or landmark
  • Add photos: Visual evidence gets better responses
  • Follow up: Check the Dashboard tab for updates
  • Stay respectful: Professional tone gets better engagement
